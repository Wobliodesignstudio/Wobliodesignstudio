Project Introduction
I designed the MVP of a mobile application tailored for a specific village community. The app allows users to create accounts, share posts (images or text), and advertise local services. It also includes features to explore town communities, list and book services such as plumbing or carpentry, and access various investment schemes. This app connects the villagers, enhances local commerce, and promotes community engagement.

[Problem]
The Problem or Challenge
The village community lacks a unified digital platform for local communication, service listings, and economic growth. The client needed a solution that would address the following challenges
Limited connectivity
Villagers struggled to easily connect with service providers like plumbers, carpenters, and electricians.
Scattered information
Knowledge about local communities and investment opportunities was not easily accessible in one place.
Service booking
There was no streamlined system for booking services or advertising local businesses.
The goal was to create an easy-to-use app that would bring the entire village together, promoting both social interaction and economic development.
