[Overview]
Project Introduction
I designed a mobile healthcare app that empowers users to manage various aspects of their health. The app offers features like a food log diary, lab report tracking, health assessments, and appointment booking with doctors. It also includes detailed vitals logging, personalized diet and workout plan creation. My role involved creating an intuitive user interface and experience that simplifies health management for users while ensuring seamless navigation across multiple functions.

[Problem]
The Problem or Challenge
We need a mobile app that makes health management easier for users but right now, our users face several challenges
  • Users find it difficult to track their daily food intake and log their vitals accurately.
  • There’s no integrated system for them to view lab reports, health assessments, or book doctor appointments in one place.
  • They want personalized workout and diet plans but don’t know where to start.
  • Our current system lacks intuitive navigation, making it hard for users to interact with different health features.
We need a solution that addresses these pain points while keeping the user experience simple and seamless.
[Solution]
The Solution
To address these challenges, I designed an intuitive mobile app that simplifies health management through
  • A unified dashboard where users can track their daily vital logs and calories calculation.
  • A streamlined appointment booking system and align with smart health assessment form so the doctor can get the exact idea about the symptoms.
  • Integrated tools for users to create personalized diet and workout plans based on their health data.
  • A user-friendly interface that caters to both experienced and novice users, ensuring smooth navigation and engagement.

[For Future]
Recommendations and next steps
To elevate the app’s functionality, I recommend integrating AI-driven smart meal and workout planning. By analyzing user inputs—such as health goals, dietary preferences, and vitals—AI can generate personalized meal plans and adaptive workout routines. This will provide users with tailored health solutions, increasing both engagement and overall app value.
