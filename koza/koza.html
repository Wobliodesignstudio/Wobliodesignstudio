Project Introduction
I designed an e-commerce platform tailored for B2B semi-premium salon owners across India. The platform allows salons to order essential products like electronics, toiletries, and other salon necessities at competitive prices. My focus was on creating a user-friendly interface that simplifies bulk ordering, ensures seamless navigation, and enhances the overall buying experience for salon businesses.

[Solution]
The Solution
To address these challenges, I designed a B2B e-commerce platform that simplifies the ordering process for salon owners:
  • A clean, intuitive interface that allows easy browsing and ordering of a wide range of salon essentials.
  • Streamlined bulk ordering functionality with features like quick reordering and order history management.
  • Categorized product listings for easy navigation, ensuring that salon owners can quickly find both electronics and toiletries.
  • Competitive pricing displays and offers tailored to the salon industry's needs, creating a seamless shopping experience.
